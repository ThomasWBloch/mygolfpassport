**`Blommenslyst Golfklub` → Consider: `Odense Blommenslyst Golfklub`** (Medium)

Golfklub" and their Facebook page uses the same prefix. DGU / Albrecht
Guide list them as just "Blommenslyst Golfklub". Both forms are in active
use; the "Odense" prefix is the club's self-preferred name.

**`Comwell Keller Park` → Consider: `Kellers Park Golf Club`** (Medium)
Two issues: (a) "Keller" should be "Kellers" (possessive form on their
site/domain kellersparkgolfclub.dk); (b) the club's own marketing drops
"Comwell" in favor of just "KellersPark Golf Club". Comwell is the
affiliated hotel, not the club name. GolfPass uses "Comwell Kellers Park
Golf Club" but the club itself uses "KellersPark Golf Club". Suggest
`Kellers Park Golf Club`.

**`Fanø Golf Links` → Consider: `Fanø Vesterhavsbads Golfklub`** (Medium)
The website title is "Fanø Vesterhavsbads Golfklub" — this is the formal
club name. "Fanø Golf Links" is the course brand (Denmark's only true
links course). Leaving as-is is defensible since the course brand is
widely known; changing makes it consistent with other clubs that carry
the "Golfklub" form.
